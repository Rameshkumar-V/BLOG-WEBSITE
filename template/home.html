Hello I'm Tamilmani, an English professor

Dr Tamilmani K T is an Associate Professor & Head, Dept of English and Vice-Principal at Nehru Memorial College in Puthanampatti, Tiruchirappalli, affiliated to Bharathidasan University, Tamil Nadu, India. He has completed 29 years of collegiate service. His research interests include American Literature and English Language Teaching. He completed a UGC funded minor research project titled “Developing Effective Strategies for Enriching the Word Power of the First-Generation Rural Learners.” He has published 30 research papers in reputed journals. He has served as a resource person for a number of academic programs in UGC-Human Resource Development Centre of Bharathidasan University.
